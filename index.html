Intro

A 4th year graduate student at the University of Colorado Denver Computer Science Department advised by Prof. Gita Alaghband. My research scope encompasses deep learning and its application to computer vision.In particular I'm interested in characterizing the inner workings of deep convolutional networks (CNN) for visual recognition tasks. My current active research subject is characterizing feature maps learned by CNN using a technique called Controlled Convolution. See projects for detail.

Introduction

Consciousness is digital. Consciousness is an information system. Consciousness is an interpreter. You are a consciousness.You are a unit of consciousness amongst many. The universe (or more generally any reality) is statistically computed virtual reality. Consciousness streams and interprets data. Consciousness is a multi-faceted sequential interpreter having functions such as streaming and synthesizing data. Consciousness builds its world view from data. Consciousness paints personalities from data. Consciousness evolves by reducing its entropy, where entropy is a measure of randomness of a given information system. A high entropy information system has more random processes than those processes that produce consistent profitable content. Hence, high entropy systems like consciousness are susceptible to de-evolution and ultimately disappearance (going back to randomness). High entropy consciousness has low chance of survival while low entropy consciousness can propagate itself by bootstrapping process towards a more resilient and fault tolerant configuration (evolve). Evolution of consciousness is enabled by a most fundamental process we know as awareness. Awareness is an emergent phenomenon that can be described as an ability to discern between at least two distinct states. We as individuals and collectives are components of a larger system with similar attributes and take part in such an optimization effort by playing decision games within an evolving virtual physical reality (VPRG) whose starting point is synonymous to a big digital bang (BDB). BDB is a metaphor that describes the conception of an open-ended computation process within a constrained environment having pre-specified initial conditions and a finite set of data points whose interactions are governed by rules of computation like those recipes found in Physics and Mathematics. A consciousness unit has an open-ended potential dictated by discrete choices and a decision space that is a function of the unitsâ€™ capabilities. The ever-unquenched human thirst to become more is an experience that describes the attitude necessary for the survival of consciousness. Love, moral, and ethical values are utilities to reduce collective entropy and allow for sustainability and evolution of a complex social system. These values align with the rule-sets by which communication between individual and groups of individuals is optimal. The outcome of Love-based , cooperative society of consciousness units is a resilient, low entropy social configuration, while one that is based on values opposite to Love are susceptible to disappearance.
